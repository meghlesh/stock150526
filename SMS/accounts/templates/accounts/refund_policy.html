REFUND & CANCELLATION POLICY


At CWS Inventory, we are committed to providing a transparent and reliable service experience to our customers. This Refund & Cancellation Policy explains the terms related to free trial access, plan purchases, cancellations, refunds, and payment processing.

Free Trial Access

  • Users are provided access to the Business Portal during the free trial period for evaluation and testing purposes.
  • The free trial is offered without any charges.

Subscription & Access Policy

  • CWS Inventory does not provide monthly subscription plans.
  • After the free trial period ends, access to the Business Portal will be automatically disabled.
  • To continue using the Business Portal, customers must purchase a valid plan through the platform.
  • Access to services will be activated only after successful payment confirmation.

Payment Policy

  • All payments for plan purchases are processed securely through Razorpay payment gateway.
  • Customers are responsible for ensuring that the payment details provided are accurate and valid.
  • Upon successful payment, the purchased plan and corresponding access will be activated as per the selected plan terms.

Payment Failure Policy

  • In case a payment transaction fails but the amount is deducted from the customer’s bank account, the refund process will be governed by the policies of the respective bank and payment gateway provider.
  • Failed transaction amounts are generally refunded automatically to the original payment method within 5–7 business days, depending on the bank or payment provider.
  • If the refund is not received within the expected timeframe, customers are advised to contact their bank or payment service provider.

Cancellation Policy

  • Customers may choose not to continue after the free trial period without any cancellation charges.
  • If a purchased plan expires and is not renewed or repurchased, access to the Business Portal will remain suspended until a new plan is purchased.
  • Purchased plans cannot be cancelled once activated.

Refund Policy

  • Since users are provided with a free trial period to evaluate the platform before purchasing, all payments made for purchased plans are generally non-refundable.
  • No refunds will be provided once a plan has been successfully purchased and activated.
  • Refund requests, if any, will be reviewed only under exceptional circumstances and solely at the discretion of management.

Contact Us

For any questions regarding cancellations, or support, please contact us through our official website or email us at info@cwsinventory.in.
